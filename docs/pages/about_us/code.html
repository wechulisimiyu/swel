Our Identity

Advancing Equity in Surgical Care.

The Surgical Work and Equity Lab (SWEL) is an interdisciplinary research collective dedicated to dismantling systemic barriers in global surgery and healthcare leadership.

The Challenge we address.

public

Global Surgical Inequity

Over 5 billion people lack access to safe, affordable surgical care. We analyze the structural determinants that perpetuate this disparity in low-resource settings.

diversity_3

Leadership Disparities

Gender and ethnic representation in surgical leadership remains stagnant. Our work targets the biases that exclude talented surgeons from decision-making roles.

lightbulb
Mission

To bridge the gap between surgical innovation and social justice through rigorous peer-reviewed evidence.

Vision

A world where surgical precision is a human right, not a privilege of geography or gender.

Strategic Approach

Translating data into structural change.

01

Quantitative Research

Utilizing large-scale data sets to map surgical outcomes and workforce demographics across international borders.

02

Policy Advocacy

Engaging with institutional leaders to implement evidence-based equity frameworks within hospital systems and academic centers.

03

Capacity Building

Empowering local investigators through mentorship and resource sharing to lead research in their own communities.
